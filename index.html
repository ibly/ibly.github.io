How we work

IBLY aims at having a simple perspective of evolution through a funnel of innovation.

  • Ideation

    Bringing ideas together - internally and externally

    At this point, the objective is to bring ideas together and think through them, by brainstorming and debating over concepts and potencial, in order to make sense of the raw idea and understand, on a first stage, if it makes sense or not. This is applied for both internal ideas as well as for ideas brought to us by other entities, such as companies, universities or individuals.

  • R&D

    Making Sense of Ideas

    After an initial brainstorming, it is time to analyze the market, by getting information and understand the target market - does it really solve a problem or gives an extra edge to the potencial clients? Is there already a solution good enough or is there space for improvement or innovation?

  • Making Products

    Making ideas into actual marketable products

    With an effective idea of the Market, it is time to structure the idea based on the initial analysis and market knowledge, as well as talking with as many stakeholders as possible - from potencial clients to partners - in order to get a "feel" of the market and to allow prioritised market focused product.

  • Making Businesses

    Minimum Viable Product, Pilot and Growth

    With the product well-structured, with market feedback, now it is time to test the product in the market and iterate from there to adapt the product and start business growth.
